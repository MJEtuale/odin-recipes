Chow Mein

Desciption

For a fully packed dinner to give you the satisfaction you want from a dinner, look no further than the 'Island Styled Chow Mein'!

Quick and easy, even my 10 year-old can cook this one, and it's sure to put grins on the faces of your team!!!

Ingredients

  • Rice oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 clove of chopped garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • 1 chopped cabbage
  • 4 x diced carrots
  • 3 x sliced capsicums
  • 1.5kg of diced lamb
  • 2 x tbsp of corn starch

Steps:

  1. Place lamb in a pan and fill with water till all the lamb is covered
  2. Once all water has dissolved and lamb is getting tender, add rice oil into the pan and saute the onions, garlic, and carrots with the lamb.
  3. Add 2 x tbsp of corn starch to the pan and mix well.
  4. When carrots are starting to get tender, add in your cabbage, capsicum, and soy sauce. Stir and mix.
  5. Add 2 x cups of water till there is enough broth to eat with rice when done.
  6. Taste and season with salt and pepper till satisfied
  7. Serve 'em up with a bowl of rice and boom, you got Chow Mein 2 Go!
